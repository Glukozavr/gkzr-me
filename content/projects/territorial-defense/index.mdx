In Territorial Defense: The Game the peaceful cities are invaded with the enemy.
Once peaceful people walking, making business, drinking coffee and flirting with colleagues now forced to
abandon whatever they were doing and defend their life with what they could find - they did not plan to fight yesterday.
Wave after wave the occupants send tanks to take hold of city centers and replace the government
with their puppets and subdue population to it's will, while local businessmen and professional barista crafting
dangerous cocktails and putting tanks on fire.
No certification or previous experience needed.
